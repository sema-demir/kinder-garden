Kindergarten Classroom And Activity Area

The Kindergarten at offers a well-rounded early childhood education with a wide range of activities to excite and inspire our youngest scholars. The KG environment nurtures a child's innate curiosity, allowing him or her to explore education in a unique and personalized way. We maintain small classroom sizes in order for our teachers and teachers' assistants to provide individual care and attention to each child's specific needs.

Our Educational Vision

The KG curriculum solidifies academic foundations of reading and writing in both English and Arabic. All learning is integrated with the most advanced technology. Interactive Boards, which are in all classrooms, and iPads with specialized applications challenge our students while stimulating their imaginations and creativity.
